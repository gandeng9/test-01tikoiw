Indeedee has two abilities, Inner Focus and Synchronize. Inner Focus prevents Indeedee from flinching when attacked, while Synchronize passes any status conditions inflicted on Indeedee to the opposing Pokémon.

Indeedee's moveset includes a variety of psychic and normal type moves, including Dazzling Gleam, Psychic, Hyper Voice, and Expanding Force. These moves make Indeedee a formidable opponent in battles.
