Job In Maldives: Security Guard

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

If you are looking for a job in Maldives, security guard might be a good option for you. As a security guard, you will be responsible for maintaining the safety and security of the premises you are assigned to. In this article, we will discuss what it takes to become a security guard in Maldives and what the job entails.

Requirements

To become a security guard in Maldives, you must be at least 18 years old and have a high school diploma or equivalent. You must also be physically fit and able to pass a medical examination. Additionally, you will need to pass a background check and obtain a security guard license from the Maldives Police Service.

Training

Before you can start working as a security guard, you will need to complete a training program. The training program will cover topics such as security procedures, emergency response, and first aid. The duration of the training program varies depending on the employer, but it typically takes a few weeks to complete.

Duties

As a security guard, your duties will include monitoring the premises, checking for unauthorized access, responding to emergencies, and reporting any incidents to the appropriate authorities. You will also be responsible for maintaining a safe and secure environment for the people on the premises.

Salary

The salary for a security guard in Maldives varies depending on the employer and the location. On average, a security guard can expect to earn between MVR 7,000 and MVR 10,000 per month. Some employers may offer additional benefits, such as health insurance and paid time off.

Advancement

If you are interested in advancing your career as a security guard, there are several options available. You can become a supervisor or manager, or you can specialize in a particular area, such as cyber security or executive protection.

Conclusion

A job as a security guard in Maldives can be a rewarding career choice. It requires dedication, training, and a commitment to maintaining the safety and security of the people and premises you are responsible for. If you are interested in pursuing a career in security, consider becoming a security guard in Maldives.
