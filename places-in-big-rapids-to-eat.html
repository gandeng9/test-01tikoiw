Top 10 Places To Eat In Big Rapids In 2023

Introduction

Big Rapids is a charming city located in the heart of Michigan. It is famous for its beautiful natural surroundings, great universities, and fantastic food. If you're a foodie visiting Big Rapids, you'll be spoilt for choices. In this article, we'll take you on a culinary journey and show you some of the best places to eat in Big Rapids in 2023.

1. Blue Cow Cafe

The Blue Cow Cafe is a cozy little place that serves up some of the best breakfast and lunch options in the city. They have a great selection of coffee, tea, and juices to accompany their delicious sandwiches, wraps, and salads. Their ingredients are always fresh, and their service is exceptional. Be sure to try their famous blueberry pancakes and their avocado toast.

2. Raven Brewing and BBQ

If you're a fan of barbecue, then Raven Brewing and BBQ is the place to go. They have a wide variety of smoked meats, including brisket, pulled pork, and ribs. They also serve up some great sides, such as mac and cheese, baked beans, and coleslaw. Their beer selection is also fantastic, with a rotating list of craft beers that will satisfy any beer lover.

3. Bluefish Kitchen + Bar

The Bluefish Kitchen + Bar is a modern restaurant that specializes in seafood. Their menu changes seasonally, but they always have fresh catches of the day. They also have a great selection of cocktails and wine to pair with your meal. Be sure to try their lobster roll and their clam chowder, both of which are some of the best in the city.

4. Cranker's Brewery

Cranker's Brewery is a local brewery that also serves up some great food. Their menu features classic pub fare, such as burgers, sandwiches, and wings, as well as some unique options, such as their fried pickles and their pretzel bites. Their beer selection is also fantastic, with a wide variety of styles to choose from. Be sure to try their Peanut Butter Porter, which is a fan favorite.

5. Falconhead Bar and Grill

Falconhead Bar and Grill is a sports bar that serves up some great food and drinks. They have a wide variety of burgers, sandwiches, and appetizers to choose from, as well as a great selection of beer and cocktails. They also have TVs throughout the bar, so it's a great place to watch a game with friends.

6. The Raven's Nest

The Raven's Nest is a cozy little restaurant that serves up some great comfort food. Their menu features classic dishes, such as meatloaf, chicken pot pie, and mac and cheese. They also have a great selection of desserts, including their famous apple pie. Their service is always friendly, and the atmosphere is warm and inviting.

7. Nawal's Mediterranean Eatery

If you're in the mood for some Mediterranean food, then Nawal's Mediterranean Eatery is the place to go. They have a wide variety of dishes, including falafel, shawarma, and hummus. Their ingredients are always fresh, and their portions are generous. They also have a great selection of vegetarian and vegan options.

8. Schuberg's Bar and Grill

Schuberg's Bar and Grill is a local favorite that serves up some great food and drinks. Their menu features classic dishes, such as burgers, sandwiches, and salads, as well as some unique options, such as their pulled pork nachos. Their beer selection is also fantastic, with a rotating list of local and regional craft beers.

9. Mancino's Pizza and Grinders

Mancino's Pizza and Grinders is a great place to go if you're in the mood for some pizza or a sub. Their menu features a wide variety of options, including classic pizzas, specialty pizzas, and grinders. Their ingredients are always fresh, and their portions are generous. They also have a great selection of salads and appetizers.

10. La Taqueria El Tapatio

La Taqueria El Tapatio is a fantastic place to go if you're in the mood for some authentic Mexican food. Their menu features a wide variety of dishes, including tacos, burritos, and quesadillas. Their ingredients are always fresh, and their portions are generous. They also have a great selection of margaritas and other cocktails.

Conclusion

Big Rapids is a great place to go if you're a foodie. There are so many great restaurants to choose from, each with its own unique atmosphere and menu. Whether you're in the mood for some barbecue, seafood, or pizza, you're sure to find something that will satisfy your cravings. So, the next time you're in Big Rapids, be sure to check out some of these fantastic places to eat.
