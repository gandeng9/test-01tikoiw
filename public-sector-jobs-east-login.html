What is Public Sector Jobs East?

Public Sector Jobs East is a job board that advertises vacancies across the public sector in the Eastern region of the UK. It's used by local authorities, the NHS, schools, and other public sector organizations to recruit staff. The platform is free to use, and job seekers can create an account to apply for jobs and receive alerts for new vacancies.

How to Create an Account

To create an account, go to the Public Sector Jobs East website and click on the "Register" button. You'll need to provide some basic information, such as your name, email address, and a password. Once you've filled in the form, click on the "Register" button, and you'll receive an email with a link to activate your account.

How to Log In

To log in, go to the Public Sector Jobs East website and click on the "Login" button. Enter your email address and password, and click on the "Login" button. If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgotten your password?" link, and follow the instructions to reset it.

How to Search for Jobs

To search for jobs, go to the Public Sector Jobs East website and click on the "Search Jobs" button. You can use the search bar to enter keywords, or you can browse by category or location. Once you've found a job you're interested in, click on the "Apply" button to start your application.

How to Create a Job Alert

To create a job alert, go to the Public Sector Jobs East website and click on the "Job Alerts" button. Enter your search criteria, such as keywords, location, and job category, and click on the "Create Alert" button.
