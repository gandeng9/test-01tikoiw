Introduction

Steve Jobs was an American businessman, inventor, and industrial designer who co-founded Apple Inc. He is widely regarded as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution and for his innovative contributions to the technology industry. However, did you know that his life and achievements have been chronicled in a best-selling biography? In this article, we will explore the Hindi version of the book and discover more about Jobs' life.

Early Life

Jobs was born in San Francisco in 1955 to Joanne Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali, two unmarried graduate students. He was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs, who raised him in Mountain View, California. Jobs' interest in electronics started at a young age, and he and his friend Steve Wozniak would tinker with gadgets in the Jobs family garage.

Career

Jobs co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976 with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. Apple's first product was the Apple I personal computer, which was followed by the Apple II. The company went public in 1980, and Jobs became a multimillionaire. He left Apple in 1985 after a power struggle with the board of directors but returned in 1997 as CEO. Jobs oversaw the development of a string of successful products, including the iMac, iPod, iPhone, and iPad.

Personal Life

Jobs was known for his unconventional personal life, which included experimenting with psychedelic drugs, practicing Zen Buddhism, and traveling to India. He was also known for his perfectionism and demanding work ethic, which sometimes put a strain on his relationships with colleagues and family members. Jobs was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer in 2003 and passed away in 2011 at the age of 56.

The Book

The biography of Steve Jobs was written by Walter Isaacson and was published in 2011, shortly after Jobs' death. The book is based on over 40 interviews with Jobs himself, as well as interviews with family members, colleagues, and competitors. The book was a best-seller and has been translated into many languages, including Hindi. The Hindi version of the book is titled "Steve Jobs: Ek Jeevan Katha" and was published in 2012 by Prabhat Prakashan.

Conclusion

Steve Jobs was a visionary who left an indelible mark on the technology industry. His life and achievements have been chronicled in a best-selling biography, which has been translated into many languages, including Hindi. "Steve Jobs: Ek Jeevan Katha" is a must-read for anyone interested in the life of this remarkable man.
