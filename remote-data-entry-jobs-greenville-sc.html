What is Data Entry?

Data entry is the process of entering data into a computer database or spreadsheet. This can include entering customer information, sales data, or any other type of numerical or text-based information. Data entry jobs are often considered entry-level positions, but they can also be a great way to gain experience in a specific industry or field.
