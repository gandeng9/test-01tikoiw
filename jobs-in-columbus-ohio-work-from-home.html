Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has reshaped the way we work. Working from home has become a new normal for many people, and it is likely to stay for a while. Columbus, Ohio, is no exception. Many companies in Columbus, Ohio, are offering work from home opportunities to their employees. In this article, we will discuss the top work from home jobs in Columbus, Ohio, and how to find them.

Top Work from Home Jobs in Columbus Ohio

1. Customer Service Representative

Customer service representatives play a vital role in any business. They are responsible for handling customer inquiries, resolving customer complaints, and providing excellent customer service. Many companies in Columbus, Ohio, are hiring customer service representatives to work from home. The average salary for a customer service representative in Columbus, Ohio, is $15 per hour.

2. Virtual Assistant

Virtual assistants are in high demand these days. They provide administrative support to businesses and individuals from a remote location. Virtual assistants can perform a wide range of tasks, including data entry, social media management, email management, and scheduling appointments. The average salary for a virtual assistant in Columbus, Ohio, is $16 per hour.

3. Online Tutor

Online tutoring is another popular work from home job in Columbus, Ohio. Online tutors provide academic assistance to students from a remote location. They can teach a wide range of subjects, including math, science, and English. The average salary for an online tutor in Columbus, Ohio, is $20 per hour.

4. Content Writer

Content writers are responsible for creating engaging and informative content for websites, blogs, and social media platforms. Many companies in Columbus, Ohio, are hiring content writers to work from home. The average salary for a content writer in Columbus, Ohio, is $25 per hour.

How to Find Work from Home Jobs in Columbus Ohio

1. Job Boards

Job boards are a great place to start your job search. There are several job boards that specialize in work from home jobs, such as FlexJobs, Remote.co, and We Work Remotely. You can search for work from home jobs in Columbus, Ohio, on these job boards.

2. Company Websites

Many companies in Columbus, Ohio, are offering work from home opportunities to their employees. You can visit the company websites and check their career pages for work from home jobs.

3. Networking

Networking is a powerful tool when it comes to finding work from home jobs. You can reach out to your professional contacts and let them know that you are looking for work from home opportunities in Columbus, Ohio. They may be able to refer you to a company that is hiring.

Conclusion

Work from home jobs are on the rise in Columbus, Ohio. Customer service representative, virtual assistant, online tutor, and content writer are some of the top work from home jobs in Columbus, Ohio. You can find work from home jobs in Columbus, Ohio, on job boards, company websites, and through networking.
