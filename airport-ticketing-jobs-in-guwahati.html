Airport Ticketing Jobs In Guwahati: Opportunities And Requirements In 2023

The Growth of Aviation Industry in Guwahati

Guwahati, the largest city in the Northeastern region of India, has become a hub for the aviation industry in recent years. With the expansion of the Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport and the opening of new domestic and international routes, the demand for airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati has increased significantly. In this article, we will explore the opportunities and requirements for those who want to pursue a career in this field.

The Role of Airport Ticketing Staff

Airport ticketing staff are responsible for providing excellent customer service to passengers, issuing and re-issuing tickets, handling reservations and changes in flight itineraries, checking in passengers, and ensuring their safety and comfort during the entire travel process. They also need to have a good understanding of the airline industry, airport procedures, and regulations.

The Qualifications and Skills Required

To qualify for airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati, candidates need to have a minimum of a high school diploma or equivalent. They should also have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, be able to work under pressure, and have a positive attitude towards customers. Knowledge of multiple languages, especially English and Assamese, is an added advantage.

The Training and Certification Process

Most airlines and airport operators provide on-the-job training to new employees to help them gain the required skills and knowledge. However, some airlines may require candidates to undergo a certification course in airport ticketing and reservations. These courses can range from a few weeks to a few months and cover topics such as customer service, reservations systems, and airport security.

The Salary and Benefits

The salary for airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati can vary depending on the airline or airport operator. However, the average starting salary is around INR 15,000-20,000 per month. Other benefits may include health insurance, travel allowances, and retirement plans. With experience and seniority, employees can expect to earn higher salaries and promotions to supervisory or managerial positions.

The Career Growth Opportunities

Airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati are not just entry-level positions. With the right skills, experience, and qualifications, employees can move up the ladder to higher-paying and more challenging roles. These may include senior ticketing agents, reservation supervisors, airport managers, or even airline executives. The aviation industry is constantly evolving, providing ample opportunities for career growth and advancement.

The Challenges and Rewards of the Job

Working in airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati can be both challenging and rewarding. On the one hand, employees need to deal with different types of passengers, some of whom may be difficult or demanding. On the other hand, they get to interact with people from different cultures and backgrounds, learn new skills, and contribute to the smooth functioning of the aviation industry.

The Importance of Customer Service in Airport Ticketing

One of the key aspects of airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati is providing excellent customer service. This means being polite, friendly, and helpful to passengers, even in stressful situations. Good customer service can lead to higher customer satisfaction, repeat business, and positive reviews, which can benefit both the employee and the airline or airport operator.

The Future of Airport Ticketing Jobs in Guwahati

With the growth of the aviation industry in Guwahati and the Northeastern region, there is a high demand for skilled and qualified airport ticketing staff. This trend is likely to continue in the coming years, as more airlines and airport operators expand their operations in the region. Therefore, airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati can provide a stable and fulfilling career for those who are interested in the aviation industry.

Conclusion

In conclusion, airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati offer a promising career option for those who have the required skills, qualifications, and attitude. With proper training and experience, employees can grow and advance in their roles and contribute to the growth of the aviation industry in the region. So, if you are looking for a career that is challenging, rewarding, and has good growth prospects, consider airport ticketing jobs in Guwahati.
