Jobs In Airport Ground Staff Guwahati

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

If you are looking for a career in the aviation industry, then jobs in airport ground staff in Guwahati can be a great opportunity for you. The airport is one of the busiest in the northeast region of India, and with the increasing number of passengers, the demand for ground staff is also rising.

Roles and Responsibilities

Airport ground staff is responsible for providing assistance to passengers, checking their tickets, and helping them with their luggage. They also ensure that the flights are on time and coordinate with other airport departments to ensure smooth operations. Ground staff also assist in the boarding process and provide safety instructions to passengers.

Qualifications and Skills

To get a job in airport ground staff in Guwahati, you need to have a minimum qualification of 10+2. However, a degree in hospitality, travel, or tourism can be an added advantage. Good communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and a customer-centric approach are some of the essential skills required for this job.

Job Opportunities

The airport ground staff job in Guwahati is not limited to a particular role. There are various departments, including customer service, security, and cargo, which offer different job opportunities. With time and experience, ground staff can also move up the ladder to supervisory or managerial positions.

Salary and Benefits

The salary of airport ground staff in Guwahati varies depending on the department and position. However, the starting salary is around INR 15,000 to 20,000 per month. The job also comes with benefits such as medical insurance, travel discounts, and retirement benefits.

Training and Development

Most airport authorities provide training to their ground staff to enhance their skills and provide a better experience to passengers. The training includes customer service, safety procedures, and technical skills. Apart from this, there are also opportunities for on-the-job training and skill development courses.

Challenges

Working in the aviation industry can be challenging due to the fast-paced environment and the need to handle difficult situations. Ground staff may have to work long hours and during odd hours, including night shifts and weekends.

Conclusion

Jobs in airport ground staff in Guwahati can be a great career opportunity for those interested in the aviation industry. With the increasing number of passengers, the demand for skilled ground staff is also rising. The job offers good salary packages, benefits, and opportunities for career growth. However, it also comes with challenges that require dedication and a customer-centric approach to overcome.
