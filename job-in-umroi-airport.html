Job In Umroi Airport: Opportunities And Requirements

Introduction

If you are looking for a career in the aviation industry, then the Umroi airport in Meghalaya, India, is a great place to start. The airport, which is located approximately 30 km from the state capital, Shillong, has a growing demand for skilled professionals in various departments. In this article, we will discuss the job opportunities available at Umroi airport and the requirements to get hired.

Job Opportunities

Umroi airport offers a wide range of job opportunities for both experienced and fresh candidates. Some of the popular job roles available at the airport include:

1. Air Traffic Controller

Air traffic controllers are responsible for managing the flow of air traffic and ensuring the safety of all flights. This is a highly demanding job that requires excellent communication and analytical skills.

2. Ground Staff

Ground staff members are responsible for various tasks, including baggage handling, cargo operations, and aircraft maintenance. This is a physically demanding job that requires good health and fitness.

3. Security Personnel

Security personnel are responsible for maintaining the safety and security of the airport. This job requires excellent communication and problem-solving skills.

Requirements

To get hired at Umroi airport, you need to meet certain requirements. Some of the common requirements for the different job roles include:

1. Educational Qualification

Most of the job roles at Umroi airport require a minimum educational qualification of 10+2 or a bachelor's degree in a relevant field.

2. Age Limit

The age limit for different job roles at Umroi airport varies. Generally, the age limit is between 18 to 30 years.

3. Physical Fitness

Most of the job roles at Umroi airport require good health and fitness. Candidates need to pass a medical examination to ensure they are physically fit.

4. Communication Skills

Excellent communication skills are a must for most of the job roles at Umroi airport. Candidates need to be fluent in English and the local language.

Conclusion

Umroi airport offers excellent job opportunities for candidates looking for a career in the aviation industry. The airport provides a challenging and rewarding work environment that is perfect for those who are passionate about their work. If you meet the requirements and have the necessary skills, then a job at Umroi airport may be the perfect fit for you.
