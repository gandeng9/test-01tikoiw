The Best Part Time Mba Programs In New York In 2023

Introduction

New York City is known for its fast-paced lifestyle, diverse culture, and bustling business scene. As such, it has become a hub for MBA programs, attracting students from all over the world. Part-time MBA programs are particularly popular among working professionals who wish to advance their careers. In this article, we will examine some of the best part-time MBA programs in New York in 2023.

Columbia Business School

Columbia Business School is consistently ranked among the top business schools in the world. Its part-time MBA program is designed to accommodate the schedules of working professionals, with classes held on evenings and weekends. The program offers a rigorous curriculum, covering topics such as finance, management, and marketing. Students can also take advantage of the school's vast network of alumni and industry connections.

New York University Stern School of Business

New York University Stern School of Business is another top-ranked business school located in the heart of New York City. Its part-time MBA program is designed for working professionals who wish to continue working while pursuing their degree. The program offers flexible scheduling options, including evening and weekend classes. Students can choose from a variety of specializations, including finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

Baruch College Zicklin School of Business

Baruch College Zicklin School of Business is a public institution located in Manhattan. Its part-time MBA program is designed for working professionals who wish to pursue their degree without interrupting their career. The program offers a flexible schedule, with classes held on evenings and weekends. The curriculum covers a wide range of business topics, including accounting, finance, and management.

Fordham University Gabelli School of Business

Fordham University Gabelli School of Business is located in the Bronx and offers a part-time MBA program designed for working professionals. The program offers flexible scheduling options, including evening and weekend classes. Students can choose from a variety of specializations, including finance, marketing, and management. The school also offers opportunities for international study and experiential learning.

Conclusion

In conclusion, New York City offers a wealth of opportunities for working professionals who wish to pursue a part-time MBA degree. The schools mentioned in this article are among the best in the city, offering rigorous curriculums, flexible scheduling options, and extensive alumni networks. Whether you are looking to advance your career or make a career change, pursuing a part-time MBA in New York is an excellent choice.
