Jobs In Saudi Arabia: The Pros And Cons Of Being A House Driver

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Living and working in Saudi Arabia can be a great opportunity for many people, especially for those who are looking to earn a good salary and experience a new culture. One of the most popular jobs for expats in Saudi Arabia is being a house driver. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of being a house driver in Saudi Arabia, and provide some tips for those considering this career.

What is a House Driver?

A house driver is a person who is employed by a family to drive them around, take care of their cars, and sometimes perform other duties, such as grocery shopping or running errands. In Saudi Arabia, it is common for wealthy families to hire house drivers, as it is considered a luxury to have a personal driver.

The Pros of Being a House Driver in Saudi Arabia

One of the biggest advantages of being a house driver in Saudi Arabia is the salary. House drivers can earn up to SAR 4,000 per month, which is a competitive salary in the region. In addition, most families provide their house drivers with accommodation, food, and sometimes even a car, which can save a lot of money. Another advantage of being a house driver in Saudi Arabia is the opportunity to experience a new culture. Saudi Arabia is a unique country with a rich history and culture, and working as a house driver can give you the chance to learn about this culture firsthand. You will also have the opportunity to meet new people and make friends, which can be a great experience.

The Cons of Being a House Driver in Saudi Arabia

One of the biggest challenges of being a house driver in Saudi Arabia is the long working hours. Most families expect their house drivers to work six days a week, with long hours each day. This can be tiring and stressful, and may not be suitable for everyone. Another challenge of being a house driver in Saudi Arabia is the cultural differences. Saudi Arabia has a conservative culture, and it is important for house drivers to respect these cultural norms. For example, it is common for women to wear an abaya (a long black cloak) in public, and men should not shake hands with women unless they are family members.

Tips for Being a Successful House Driver in Saudi Arabia

If you are considering a career as a house driver in Saudi Arabia, here are some tips to help you be successful: - Be punctual and reliable: Families expect their house drivers to be on time and reliable, so make sure you are always punctual and dependable. - Respect cultural norms: As mentioned earlier, it is important to respect the cultural norms in Saudi Arabia. Be aware of what is considered appropriate behavior and dress accordingly. - Build a good relationship with the family: Building a good relationship with the family you work for is important. Be friendly and polite, and try to anticipate their needs. - Keep the car clean and well-maintained: As a house driver, you will be responsible for taking care of the family's cars. Make sure you keep the car clean and well-maintained, as this will show that you take your job seriously. - Stay safe on the road: Saudi Arabia has a high number of traffic accidents, so it is important to be a safe driver. Always wear your seatbelt and follow traffic rules.

Conclusion

Being a house driver in Saudi Arabia can be a challenging yet rewarding career. It offers a competitive salary, the opportunity to experience a new culture, and the chance to make new friends. However, it is important to be aware of the challenges and cultural differences, and to take steps to be a successful house driver. If you are considering this career, we hope these tips will help you make an informed decision.
