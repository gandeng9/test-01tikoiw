Is Jobstreet Free For Employers?

Introduction

JobStreet is an online job platform that connects job seekers with employers. It is a popular platform in Southeast Asia, including Malaysia, Singapore, Philippines, Indonesia, and Vietnam. Many employers use JobStreet to find potential candidates for job openings. In this article, we will discuss whether JobStreet is free for employers or not.

Is JobStreet Free for Employers?

The short answer is no, JobStreet is not entirely free for employers. However, employers can still use JobStreet without paying anything. JobStreet offers two types of accounts for employers- Free and Premium. Free accounts have limited features, while Premium accounts have more features and benefits.

Free Account

Employers can sign up for a free account on JobStreet. With a free account, employers can post job openings, search for resumes, and receive applications from job seekers. However, free accounts have limitations. Employers can only post up to three job openings, and their job postings will have less visibility compared to Premium accounts. Additionally, employers cannot access advanced search filters and cannot see who viewed their company profile.

Premium Account

Employers can upgrade their account to Premium for more features and benefits. Premium accounts have various pricing plans, depending on the number of job postings and the duration of the subscription. With a Premium account, employers can post more job openings, access advanced search filters, and see who viewed their company profile. Premium accounts also have priority placement, which means their job postings will have higher visibility compared to free accounts.

Conclusion

JobStreet is not entirely free for employers, but they can still use the platform without paying anything. Free accounts have limitations, while Premium accounts have more features and benefits. Employers can choose which account to use based on their needs and budget. Whether free or Premium, JobStreet is a useful platform for employers to find potential candidates for job openings.

Tips for Employers on JobStreet

Here are some tips for employers using JobStreet:

  1. Write a clear and concise job description
  2. Use relevant keywords to make your job postings more searchable
  3. Screen resumes thoroughly to find the best candidates
  4. Personalize your communication with job seekers
  5. Offer competitive salary and benefits to attract top talent

Conclusion

In conclusion, JobStreet is a valuable platform for employers to find potential candidates for job openings. While it is not entirely free, employers can still use the platform without paying anything. Employers can choose between free and Premium accounts based on their needs and budget. By following the tips mentioned above, employers can increase their chances of finding the best candidates for their job openings.
