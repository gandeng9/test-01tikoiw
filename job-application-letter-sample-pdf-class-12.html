Introduction

Job application letter is an important document that is required when applying for a job. It is the first document that the employer sees and it is important to make a good impression. In this article, we will provide you with a sample job application letter in PDF format that is suitable for class 12 students.

Sample Job Application Letter

Sender's Information

Your Name
Your Address
City, State ZIP Code
Phone Number
Email Address

Date

Employer's Name
Company Name
Address
City, State ZIP Code

Dear Hiring Manager

I am writing to express my interest in the position of [position] at [company name]. I am a recent graduate of [school name] with a [degree] in [major]. I am excited about the opportunity to join your team and contribute my skills and knowledge to the success of your company.

Why I am a Good Fit for the Company

During my time at [school name], I gained valuable experience in [relevant experience]. I am confident that my skills and knowledge in [relevant skills] would make me a valuable addition to your team. I am a quick learner, detail-oriented, and thrive in a fast-paced environment.

Why I am Interested in the Position

I am impressed with [company name]'s commitment to [company values]. I believe that [company name] is a leader in [industry] and I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to its success. I am particularly interested in the [specific aspect of the position] and believe that my skills and experience make me a good fit for this role.

Closing

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss my candidacy further. Please find attached my resume and other relevant documents. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Sincerely,

Your Name

Conclusion

A well-written job application letter can help you stand out from other applicants and increase your chances of getting hired. We hope that this sample job application letter in PDF format will help class 12 students in their job search. Remember to customize the letter for each job application and highlight your relevant skills and experience.
