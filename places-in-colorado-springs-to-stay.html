Best Places To Stay In Colorado Springs

Introduction

Looking for the perfect place to stay in Colorado Springs? Look no further! This beautiful city nestled at the foot of the Rocky Mountains has some of the best accommodations in the state. Whether you're looking for a cozy bed and breakfast, a luxurious resort, or a budget-friendly hotel, Colorado Springs has something for everyone.

1. The Broadmoor

One of the most iconic hotels in Colorado Springs, The Broadmoor has been around since 1918. This luxurious resort boasts 779 rooms and suites, 20 restaurants and lounges, a world-class spa, and three championship golf courses. The Broadmoor is also home to a private lake, where guests can enjoy fishing, kayaking, and paddle boating.

2. The Mining Exchange

If you're looking for a more boutique-style hotel, The Mining Exchange is the perfect choice. This historic hotel, located in the heart of downtown Colorado Springs, features 117 beautifully appointed guest rooms and suites. The hotel also has a rooftop bar and restaurant, where you can enjoy stunning views of the city.

3. The Cliff House at Pikes Peak

Nestled at the base of Pikes Peak, The Cliff House is a charming Victorian-style hotel that has been around since 1873. The hotel features 54 guest rooms and suites, each with its unique decor and style. The Cliff House also has a fine dining restaurant, where you can enjoy delicious cuisine while taking in the breathtaking views of Pikes Peak.

4. The Antlers, A Wyndham Hotel

Located in the heart of downtown Colorado Springs, The Antlers is a historic hotel that has been around since 1883. The hotel features 292 guest rooms and suites, as well as an indoor pool, fitness center, and multiple restaurants and bars. The Antlers is also within walking distance of many of Colorado Springs' top attractions, including the Colorado Springs Pioneers Museum and the Colorado Springs Fine Arts Center.

5. Garden of the Gods Resort and Club

If you're looking for a luxurious retreat that's close to nature, the Garden of the Gods Resort and Club is the perfect choice. This stunning resort features 116 guest rooms and suites, as well as a world-class spa, three swimming pools, and multiple dining options. The resort is also located adjacent to the Garden of the Gods Park, where you can explore the stunning red rock formations.

6. Cheyenne Mountain Resort

Nestled at the base of Cheyenne Mountain, the Cheyenne Mountain Resort is a family-friendly resort that has something for everyone. The resort features 316 guest rooms and suites, as well as multiple dining options, a spa, and a championship golf course. The resort also has a private lake, where you can enjoy fishing, kayaking, and paddle boating.

7. The St. Mary's Inn, Bed and Breakfast

If you're looking for a cozy bed and breakfast experience, The St. Mary's Inn is the perfect choice. This charming Victorian-style inn features five guest rooms, each with its unique decor and style. The inn also serves a delicious homemade breakfast every morning, made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

8. The Colorado Springs KOA

For those looking for a more budget-friendly option, the Colorado Springs KOA is a great choice. This campground features tent and RV sites, as well as cabins and lodges. The campground also has multiple amenities, including a swimming pool, hot tub, and playground.

9. The Academy Hotel Colorado Springs

Located near the United States Air Force Academy, The Academy Hotel Colorado Springs is a great choice for families visiting the area. The hotel features 200 guest rooms and suites, as well as multiple dining options, a fitness center, and an indoor pool. The hotel also offers a complimentary shuttle service to and from the airport.

10. The Stagecoach Inn

Located in the historic town of Manitou Springs, The Stagecoach Inn is a charming hotel that has been around since 1881. The hotel features 28 guest rooms, each with its unique decor and style. The Stagecoach Inn is also located within walking distance of many of Manitou Springs' top attractions, including the Manitou Cliff Dwellings and the Pikes Peak Cog Railway.

Conclusion

Whether you're looking for a luxurious resort or a cozy bed and breakfast, Colorado Springs has something for everyone. With its stunning natural beauty and rich history, this city is the perfect destination for anyone looking for a relaxing getaway. So why wait? Book your trip to Colorado Springs today!
