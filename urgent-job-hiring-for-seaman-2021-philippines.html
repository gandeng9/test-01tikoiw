Urgent Job Hiring For Seaman 2021 Philippines

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has greatly affected the job market, especially for seafarers. However, with the gradual reopening of the economy, the demand for seafarers has increased. In the Philippines, there is an urgent job hiring for seamen in 2021. This article will discuss the current job market for seafarers in the Philippines.

Background

The maritime industry is a significant contributor to the Philippine economy, with over 400,000 Filipino seafarers working overseas. However, due to the pandemic, many seafarers lost their jobs, and new job opportunities were scarce. The government and the private sector have been working together to address this issue by creating new job opportunities for Filipino seafarers.

Job Opportunities

Despite the pandemic, the maritime industry remains a vital sector in the Philippines. There are currently several job opportunities for seafarers, including deckhands, engineers, cooks, and other positions. The demand for seafarers is expected to increase as international trade picks up, and the shipping industry recovers.

Requirements

To qualify for a seafarer position, candidates must possess the necessary skills and certifications. Seafarers must have a valid Seafarer's Identification and Record Book (SIRB), Basic Safety Training (BST) certification, and other relevant certifications. Additionally, candidates must pass a medical examination to ensure they are physically fit for the job.

Salaries and Benefits

Seafarers in the Philippines receive competitive salaries and benefits. The average monthly salary for an entry-level seafarer ranges from Php 30,000 to Php 50,000. Seafarers are also entitled to other benefits, such as health insurance, vacation leave, and retirement benefits.

How to Apply

There are several ways to apply for seafarer positions in the Philippines. Candidates can apply directly to shipping companies or through a manning agency. Manning agencies are authorized by the government to recruit, train, and deploy seafarers to different vessels worldwide.

Tips for Applicants

To increase their chances of getting hired, applicants should ensure that they have all the necessary certifications and documents. Candidates should also be physically fit and mentally prepared for the demands of the job. Additionally, applicants should research the company or manning agency they are applying to and prepare for the interview.

Conclusion

The urgent job hiring for seamen in the Philippines is a positive development for seafarers who lost their jobs due to the pandemic. The maritime industry is expected to recover, and the demand for seafarers will increase. Candidates who possess the necessary qualifications and skills should take advantage of the job opportunities available.
