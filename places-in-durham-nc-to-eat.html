Discover The Best Places In Durham Nc To Eat In 2023

Introduction

Durham, North Carolina is a foodie's paradise, with a diverse range of restaurants and cafes offering delicious cuisine from around the world. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor, you'll find an abundance of options to satisfy your cravings. In this article, we'll be exploring the top places to eat in Durham, NC in 2023.

1. Dame's Chicken and Waffles

One of the most popular places to eat in Durham, NC is Dame's Chicken and Waffles. This restaurant is known for its unique take on the classic dish, with a variety of waffle and chicken combinations to choose from. The restaurant has a cozy atmosphere and friendly staff, making it a great spot for a casual meal with friends or family.

2. Saltbox Seafood Joint

If you're a seafood lover, you won't want to miss Saltbox Seafood Joint. This restaurant serves up fresh, locally sourced seafood in a relaxed and casual setting. The menu changes daily depending on what's available, so you can always expect something new and exciting.

3. Pizzeria Toro

For some of the best pizza in town, head to Pizzeria Toro. This restaurant specializes in Neapolitan-style pizza, with a focus on high-quality ingredients and traditional techniques. The menu also includes a variety of small plates, salads, and desserts to round out your meal.

4. Bull City Burger and Brewery

Bull City Burger and Brewery is a popular spot for beer enthusiasts and burger lovers alike. The restaurant offers a range of unique burger options, including vegetarian and vegan options, as well as a selection of craft beers brewed on-site. The atmosphere is lively and welcoming, making it a great spot for a night out with friends.

5. The Parlour

If you're in the mood for something sweet, head to The Parlour for some delicious ice cream. This local favorite offers a variety of flavors, including classic options like vanilla and chocolate, as well as more unique combinations like lavender honey and blueberry buttermilk. The ice cream is made in-house using locally sourced ingredients, making it a truly Durham experience.

6. Juju

Juju is a tapas-style restaurant that offers a unique take on Asian cuisine. The menu includes a variety of small plates that are perfect for sharing with friends, as well as a selection of craft cocktails and sake. The atmosphere is lively and energetic, making it a great spot for a night out.

7. Guglhupf Bakery and Patisserie

For some of the best baked goods in town, head to Guglhupf Bakery and Patisserie. This bakery offers a variety of breads, pastries, and cakes, as well as a selection of savory options like quiches and sandwiches. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, making it a great spot for a morning coffee or afternoon treat.

8. Monuts

Monuts is a popular spot for breakfast and brunch, serving up a range of delicious dishes like bagels, sandwiches, and egg plates. The restaurant also offers a variety of baked goods, including some of the best donuts in town. The atmosphere is casual and laid-back, making it a great spot for a weekend brunch with friends.

9. Dashi

Dashi is a modern ramen shop that offers a unique take on the classic dish. The menu includes a variety of ramen options, as well as small plates and sake. The atmosphere is sleek and modern, making it a great spot for a date night or a night out with friends.

10. Luna Rotisserie and Empanadas

Luna Rotisserie and Empanadas is a Latin American restaurant that offers a variety of delicious dishes, including rotisserie chicken, empanadas, and sandwiches. The restaurant also has a selection of craft cocktails and beer to round out your meal. The atmosphere is lively and welcoming, making it a great spot for a casual dinner with friends.

Conclusion

Whether you're in the mood for seafood, pizza, or ice cream, Durham, NC has something to offer. With so many great restaurants and cafes to choose from, it's easy to see why this city is a foodie's paradise. So why not grab some friends and head out to explore the top places to eat in Durham, NC in 2023?
