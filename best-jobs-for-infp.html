What is an INFP?

INFP stands for Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, and Perceiving. These individuals are known for their creativity, empathy, and idealism. They are self-aware and have a deep understanding of their emotions and feelings. INFPs are often described as dreamers and idealists who are driven by their values and beliefs.
