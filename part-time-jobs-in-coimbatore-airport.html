Part Time Jobs In Coimbatore Airport: A Guide For Job Seekers In 2023

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Coimbatore International Airport has become a prominent airport in South India in recent years. The growth in the number of passengers and airlines has created a demand for part-time jobs at the airport. If you are a student or someone looking for additional income, working part-time at the airport can be a great option. In this article, we will discuss the various part-time job opportunities available at Coimbatore Airport.

Ground Staff

Ground staff is responsible for various tasks such as check-in, security, baggage handling, and customer service. Part-time job opportunities are available for these roles. The eligibility criteria for these roles are usually a minimum of 12th standard education and good communication skills in English and Tamil.

Retail and Food Outlets

Coimbatore Airport has a variety of retail and food outlets. These outlets require part-time employees for roles such as sales assistants, cashiers, and customer service. The eligibility criteria for these roles are usually a minimum of 10th standard education and good communication skills.

Housekeeping

The airport requires housekeeping staff to maintain the cleanliness of the terminal building. Part-time job opportunities are available for these roles. The eligibility criteria for these roles are usually a minimum of 8th standard education and basic communication skills.

Baggage Handling

Baggage handling is an essential task at the airport. Part-time job opportunities are available for baggage handlers. The eligibility criteria for these roles are usually a minimum of 10th standard education and good physical fitness.

Security

Security is a crucial aspect of airport operations. Part-time job opportunities are available for security staff. The eligibility criteria for these roles are usually a minimum of 12th standard education and good physical fitness.

Benefits of Part-Time Jobs at Coimbatore Airport

Part-time jobs at the airport offer many benefits. These benefits include flexible working hours, competitive pay, exposure to the aviation industry, and the opportunity to work with people from diverse backgrounds.

How to Apply for Part-Time Jobs at Coimbatore Airport

The airport authorities usually advertise part-time job vacancies on their official website. Interested candidates can apply online by filling out the application form and submitting it along with their resume and other required documents.

Conclusion

Coimbatore Airport offers a range of part-time job opportunities for students and job seekers looking for additional income. The eligibility criteria for these roles vary depending on the job. Part-time jobs at the airport offer many benefits and are a great way to gain exposure to the aviation industry. If you are interested in applying for part-time jobs at the airport, keep an eye on the official website for job vacancies.
