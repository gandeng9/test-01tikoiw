Jobs In Pittsburgh That Hire Felons

Introduction

Finding a job as a felon is a daunting task. It is even more challenging when you live in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Many companies have strict hiring policies that prohibit them from employing felons. However, not all hope is lost. There are still several jobs in Pittsburgh that hire felons. In this article, we will explore some of these jobs, the requirements, and where to find them.

Jobs That Hire Felons in Pittsburgh

There are several jobs in Pittsburgh that felons can apply for. These include:

1. Construction Jobs

Construction jobs are a great option for felons. Most employers in this industry do not conduct background checks. Instead, they focus on the skills and experience of the applicant. Some of the jobs available in this industry include carpentry, painting, roofing, and plumbing.

2. Restaurant Jobs

Restaurants are another great option for felons. Most restaurants in Pittsburgh are always looking for staff to work in the kitchen, cleaning, and serving. These jobs are usually entry-level and do not require any experience. However, some restaurants may conduct background checks, so it is advisable to check with the employer before applying.

3. Landscaping Jobs

Landscaping companies are always looking for people to help with lawn care, tree trimming, and other outdoor services. These jobs do not require any experience, and most employers do not conduct background checks.

4. Delivery Jobs

Delivery jobs are another great option for felons. Companies like Uber Eats, Grubhub, and DoorDash are always looking for drivers to deliver food. These jobs are flexible, and you can work on your own schedule.

Where to Find Jobs That Hire Felons in Pittsburgh

Finding a job as a felon can be a challenge. However, there are several resources that you can use to find employment opportunities. These include:

1. CareerLink Pittsburgh

CareerLink Pittsburgh is a government-run job board that lists job opportunities in Pittsburgh. They have a section for felons that lists jobs that are available to people with a criminal record.

2. Craigslist Pittsburgh

Craigslist Pittsburgh is another great resource for finding jobs that hire felons. You can search for jobs in the "jobs" section and filter by "felon-friendly."

3. Staffing Agencies

Staffing agencies are third-party companies that help employers find employees. They can help felons find jobs that are a good fit for their skills and experience.

Conclusion

Finding a job as a felon can be a challenging task. However, there are still several jobs in Pittsburgh that hire felons. Construction jobs, restaurant jobs, landscaping jobs, and delivery jobs are just a few of the options available. It is essential to check with the employer before applying to ensure that they are felon-friendly. Use resources like CareerLink Pittsburgh, Craigslist Pittsburgh, and staffing agencies to find job opportunities. With persistence and determination, you can find a job in Pittsburgh that will help you build a better future.
