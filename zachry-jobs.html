Job Positions Available at Zachry

Zachry offers a broad range of job positions for individuals with different skill sets and experience levels. Some of the job positions available at Zachry include:

Project Manager

Project managers are responsible for overseeing the entire construction process, from planning and budgeting to execution and completion. They work closely with clients, engineers, and other stakeholders to ensure that projects are completed on time, within budget, and according to specifications.

Construction Superintendent

Construction superintendents are responsible for managing all aspects of construction projects, including scheduling, budgeting, and quality control. They work closely with project managers and other team members to ensure that projects are completed safely, efficiently, and to the highest standards.

Civil Engineer

Civil engineers are responsible for designing and overseeing the construction of infrastructure projects, such as roads, bridges, and buildings. They work closely with architects, contractors, and other engineers to ensure that projects are designed and built to meet the needs of clients and communities.

Electrician

Electricians are responsible for installing and maintaining electrical systems in buildings, facilities, and other structures. They work with a wide range of electrical equipment, including wiring, lighting, and power distribution systems.
