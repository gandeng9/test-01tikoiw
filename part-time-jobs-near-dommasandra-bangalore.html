What are Part-Time Jobs?

A part-time job is a form of employment where an individual works for fewer hours than a full-time job. Part-time jobs are ideal for those who cannot commit to a full-time job due to various reasons such as studies, family responsibilities, or health issues. Part-time jobs offer flexibility and give individuals the opportunity to pursue their interests while earning a decent income.
