Career In Airport Management After 12Th

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

After completing 12th, students are often worried about their future career options. One of the most popular and lucrative career options is airport management. With the growth of the aviation industry, the demand for airport management professionals has increased significantly. In this article, we will discuss the career prospects, eligibility, and job opportunities in airport management after 12th.

Eligibility

To pursue a career in airport management, a student must have completed their 12th standard in any stream. However, some universities may require students to have studied mathematics or physics as a subject in 12th. Additionally, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in 12th to be eligible for admission.

Courses

There are various courses available for students who want to pursue a career in airport management. Some of the most popular courses include BBA in Airport Management, MBA in Airport Management, Diploma in Airport Management, and Certificate in Airport Management. These courses cover topics such as airport operations, aviation safety, air traffic control, and airport security.

Job Opportunities

After completing a course in airport management, students can work in various sectors of the aviation industry. Some of the job roles include airport manager, airport operations manager, airport security manager, air traffic controller, ground staff, and customer service executive. The salary packages for these job roles are also quite attractive, with some entry-level roles offering up to INR 6 lakhs per annum.

Career Prospects

The aviation industry is growing rapidly, and this growth is expected to continue in the coming years. This means that the demand for airport management professionals is also going to increase. According to a report by the International Air Transport Association (IATA), the number of air passengers is expected to double in the next 20 years. This will lead to the creation of numerous job opportunities for airport management professionals.

Skills Required

To excel in the field of airport management, students must possess certain skills. Some of the essential skills include excellent communication skills, leadership skills, problem-solving skills, decision-making skills, and time management skills. Additionally, students must be able to work under pressure and have a good understanding of the aviation industry.

Top Universities

There are numerous universities in India that offer courses in airport management. Some of the top universities include Indira Gandhi National Open University, University of Mumbai, Lovely Professional University, and Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research. These universities offer excellent infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry exposure to students.

Conclusion

A career in airport management after 12th is an excellent choice for students who are interested in the aviation industry. With the growth of the industry, the demand for airport management professionals is only going to increase. Students can choose from various courses and job roles and work their way up the ladder to achieve success in this field.
