What are Temp Services?

Temp services, also known as temporary staffing agencies, are businesses that provide temporary workers to other businesses. They offer a range of services, including office support, industrial staffing, and healthcare staffing.

Why Do Some Temp Services Require Drug Tests?

Drug testing is a common requirement for many jobs, including temporary positions. This is because employers want to ensure that their employees are safe to work and won't put themselves or others at risk due to drug use.
