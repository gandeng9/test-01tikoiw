Introduction

Dublin is a vibrant city that has a thriving economy, with many businesses operating in the area. As a result, there is a high demand for skilled bookkeepers who can manage financial records for these businesses. In this article, we will explore some of the top part-time bookkeeping jobs available in Dublin in 2023.

What is Bookkeeping?

Bookkeeping is the process of recording, organizing, and managing financial transactions for a business. Bookkeepers are responsible for ensuring that financial records are accurate and up-to-date, maintaining accounting ledgers, and reconciling bank statements.

Why Consider Part-Time Bookkeeping Jobs?

Part-time bookkeeping jobs are an excellent option for individuals who are looking for flexible work arrangements. These roles offer the freedom to work on a flexible schedule, which is perfect for individuals who have other commitments such as family or studies.

Top Part-Time Bookkeeping Jobs in Dublin

1. Accountant Online

Accountant Online is a leading online accounting firm that offers bookkeeping services to businesses in Dublin. They provide a range of services, including bank reconciliation, accounts payable and receivable, and financial reporting. This part-time role requires a minimum of 20 hours per week and is perfect for individuals who are looking for a long-term bookkeeping job.

2. TaxAssist Accountants

TaxAssist Accountants is a well-established accounting firm that provides bookkeeping services to small businesses in Dublin. They are currently looking for a part-time bookkeeper to join their team. The role requires a minimum of 15 hours per week and involves maintaining accounting records, preparing financial statements, and reconciling bank statements.

3. Bookkeeping Plus

Bookkeeping Plus is a boutique accounting firm that provides bookkeeping services to businesses in Dublin. They offer a range of services, including payroll management, financial reporting, and bank reconciliation. This part-time role requires a minimum of 10 hours per week and is perfect for individuals who are looking for a flexible and challenging bookkeeping job.

Skills Required for Part-Time Bookkeeping Jobs in Dublin

To be successful in part-time bookkeeping jobs in Dublin, you need to have a range of skills, including:
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Excellent organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently
  • Good communication skills
  • Proficiency in accounting software such as QuickBooks or Xero

Conclusion

Part-time bookkeeping jobs in Dublin offer flexibility, freedom, and the opportunity to work in a dynamic environment. With the right skills and experience, you can secure a role in one of the many accounting firms that operate in the city. So, if you are looking for a part-time job in Dublin, consider a career in bookkeeping.
