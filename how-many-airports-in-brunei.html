Introduction

Brunei is a small country located in Southeast Asia, with a population of just over 400,000 people. Despite its small size, Brunei has a number of airports that serve both domestic and international flights. In this article, we will explore how many airports are in Brunei.

Domestic Airports

Brunei has two domestic airports: the Brunei International Airport and the Limbang Airport. The Brunei International Airport is the main airport in the country and is located in the capital city of Bandar Seri Begawan. It serves as the hub for the national carrier, Royal Brunei Airlines, which operates flights to destinations within the country as well as to other parts of Southeast Asia. The Limbang Airport, on the other hand, is located in the nearby Malaysian state of Sarawak and serves as a key gateway for travellers between Brunei and Malaysia.

International Airports

In addition to the domestic airports, Brunei also has one international airport: the Brunei International Airport. This airport serves as the main gateway for international travellers into the country and is located just a few kilometres outside of Bandar Seri Begawan. It offers direct flights to a number of destinations in Asia and the Middle East, including Hong Kong, Dubai, and Kuala Lumpur.

Airport Infrastructure

Despite having a relatively small number of airports, Brunei has invested heavily in airport infrastructure in recent years. The Brunei International Airport, in particular, has undergone significant expansion and renovation works, including the construction of a new terminal building and runway extension. These improvements have helped to improve the airport's capacity and efficiency, making it a more attractive destination for airlines and travellers alike.

Airline Industry

The airline industry in Brunei is dominated by Royal Brunei Airlines, which is the national carrier of the country. In addition to operating domestic and international flights, the airline also provides ground handling services at the Brunei International Airport. Other airlines that operate flights to and from Brunei include AirAsia, Malaysia Airlines, and Singapore Airlines.
