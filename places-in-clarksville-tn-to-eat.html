Places In Clarksville Tn To Eat

Introduction

Clarksville, Tennessee is a city that is growing rapidly, and with that comes a growing food scene. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to eat. In this article, we will explore the best places to eat in Clarksville, TN.

1. Strawberry Alley Ale Works

Located in the heart of downtown Clarksville, Strawberry Alley Ale Works is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. They offer a wide variety of craft beers and a menu that features classic American dishes with a twist. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, with exposed brick walls and a fireplace to warm up on chilly nights.

2. The Choppin' Block

For those looking for a steakhouse experience, The Choppin' Block is the place to go. Their menu features mouth-watering steaks and seafood, as well as a selection of cocktails and wines. The ambiance is upscale and elegant, making it the perfect spot for a special occasion.

3. Nicoletta's Italian Cafe

If you're in the mood for Italian food, Nicoletta's Italian Cafe is the way to go. They serve classic Italian dishes like pasta, pizza, and calzones, as well as a selection of wines and cocktails. The atmosphere is cozy and intimate, making it a great spot for a date night.

4. Blackhorse Pub and Brewery

Blackhorse Pub and Brewery is a local favorite, known for their craft beers and pub fare. Their menu features classic pub dishes like fish and chips, as well as a variety of burgers and sandwiches. The atmosphere is lively and fun, with live music on the weekends.

5. The Looking Glass Restaurant

For a fine dining experience, The Looking Glass Restaurant is the place to go. Their menu features upscale dishes like steak and lobster, as well as a selection of wines and cocktails. The atmosphere is elegant and sophisticated, making it the perfect spot for a special occasion.

6. The Gilroy

The Gilroy is a trendy spot that offers a unique dining experience. Their menu features shareable plates and craft cocktails, making it the perfect spot for a night out with friends. The atmosphere is lively and fun, with a modern decor and a rooftop bar.

7. Roux Americajun

Roux Americajun is a fusion restaurant that combines American and Cajun cuisine. Their menu features dishes like gumbo, jambalaya, and po' boys, as well as classic American dishes like burgers and sandwiches. The atmosphere is casual and fun, with a colorful decor and a lively vibe.

8. Tennessee Valley Brewing Company

For beer lovers, Tennessee Valley Brewing Company is a must-visit spot. They offer a variety of craft beers and a menu that features classic pub fare like wings and nachos. The atmosphere is laid-back and casual, with a patio for enjoying the Tennessee weather.

9. Yada Yada Yada Deli

Yada Yada Yada Deli is a local favorite for sandwiches and salads. Their menu features classic deli sandwiches like pastrami and corned beef, as well as a selection of salads and soups. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, with a friendly staff that makes you feel at home.

10. The Downtown Commons

The Downtown Commons is a public park that features a variety of food trucks on weekends and during special events. It's the perfect spot for a casual lunch or dinner, with a variety of options to choose from. The atmosphere is laid-back and fun, with live music and a community vibe.

Conclusion

Clarksville, TN has a growing food scene, with something for everyone. Whether you're in the mood for fine dining or casual pub fare, there's a spot for you. With so many options to choose from, you'll never run out of new places to try.
