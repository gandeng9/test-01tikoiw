Introduction

If you're looking to pursue a part-time MBA program in Canada, you'll be happy to know that there are plenty of options available to you. However, with so many programs to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is the best fit for you. In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the best part-time MBA programs in Canada in 2023.

1. Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto

The Rotman School of Management at the University of Toronto offers an excellent part-time MBA program that is designed to fit the needs of working professionals. The program is flexible, allowing students to choose the pace and intensity of their studies. The curriculum is rigorous and covers a wide range of business topics, including accounting, finance, marketing, and strategy.

1.1 Program Details

The part-time MBA program at Rotman can be completed in as little as 32 months, and classes are held in the evenings and on weekends. Students can choose to specialize in one of nine areas, including financial reporting and analysis, global management, and innovation and entrepreneurship.

2. Schulich School of Business, York University

The Schulich School of Business at York University offers a part-time MBA program that is highly regarded for its academic excellence and practical focus. The program is designed to help students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in today's fast-paced business environment.

2.1 Program Details

The part-time MBA program at Schulich can be completed in as little as 24 months, and classes are held in the evenings and on weekends. The program offers a variety of specializations, including finance, marketing, and operations management.

3. Sauder School of Business, University of British Columbia

The Sauder School of Business at the University of British Columbia offers a part-time MBA program that is highly flexible and customizable. The program is designed to meet the needs of working professionals who want to enhance their skills and knowledge in business.

3.1 Program Details

The part-time MBA program at Sauder can be completed in as little as 28 months, and classes are held in the evenings and on weekends. Students can choose to specialize in one of six areas, including consulting, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

4. DeGroote School of Business, McMaster University

The DeGroote School of Business at McMaster University offers a part-time MBA program that is highly respected for its academic rigor and practical focus. The program is designed to help students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in today's competitive business environment.

4.1 Program Details

The part-time MBA program at DeGroote can be completed in as little as 28 months, and classes are held in the evenings and on weekends. Students can choose to specialize in one of five areas, including accounting, finance, and marketing.

5. Alberta School of Business, University of Alberta

The Alberta School of Business at the University of Alberta offers a part-time MBA program that is highly flexible and customizable. The program is designed to meet the needs of working professionals who want to enhance their skills and knowledge in business.

Conclusion

In conclusion, pursuing a part-time MBA program in Canada is a great way to enhance your skills and knowledge in business while still maintaining your work-life balance. The programs listed above are some of the best part-time MBA programs in Canada in 2023, and are sure to provide you with the education and training you need to succeed in your career.
