Driver Job In Airport Coimbatore: Opportunities And Requirements

Introduction

Are you looking for a driver job in Coimbatore airport? If yes, then you are at the right place. In this article, we will discuss the opportunities and requirements for a driver job in the airport.

Opportunities

The Coimbatore airport is growing rapidly, and so are the opportunities for drivers. The airport requires drivers for various purposes such as transporting passengers, cargo, and luggage. There are also opportunities for drivers in the airport's maintenance department.

Passenger Transportation

Passenger transportation is one of the primary responsibilities of a driver in the airport. The driver must have a valid driving license and be familiar with the city's roads. The driver must also be able to communicate effectively with the passengers and provide excellent customer service.

Cargo and Luggage Transportation

Another responsibility of a driver in the airport is to transport cargo and luggage. The driver must be physically fit to load and unload heavy bags and packages. The driver must also have knowledge of the airport's cargo handling procedures.

Maintenance Department

The airport's maintenance department requires drivers for various tasks such as transporting equipment and materials, performing inspections, and maintaining vehicles. The driver must have basic knowledge of vehicle maintenance and repair.

Requirements

To work as a driver in the airport, you must meet certain requirements.

Education and Experience

The minimum educational qualification for a driver job in the airport is a high school diploma. However, some employers may prefer candidates with a degree or diploma in a related field. Experience in driving and customer service is also preferred.

Driving License

A valid driving license is a must-have requirement for a driver job in the airport. The license must be issued by the Regional Transport Office (RTO) and should be in good standing.

Language Skills

The driver must have excellent communication skills in English and the local language. The driver must be able to communicate effectively with passengers, colleagues, and superiors.

Physical Fitness

The driver must be physically fit to perform tasks such as loading and unloading heavy bags and packages. The driver must also have good eyesight and hearing.

Conclusion

Working as a driver in the airport can be an exciting and rewarding job. The airport offers various opportunities for drivers, and the requirements are not too stringent. If you meet the requirements, then apply for a driver job in the Coimbatore airport today.
