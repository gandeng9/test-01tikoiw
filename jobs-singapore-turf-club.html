Jobs At Singapore Turf Club: Opportunities And Benefits

Introduction

Singapore Turf Club is one of the leading horse racing operators in Asia. It is a world-class racing and sports entertainment venue that offers a range of exciting job opportunities to people who are passionate about the racing industry. The company is committed to providing a dynamic and challenging work environment that fosters personal and professional growth.

Job Opportunities

Singapore Turf Club offers a wide range of job opportunities in various departments such as racing, operations, finance, marketing, and customer service. Some of the popular job roles include:

Racing

Assistant Trainer, Jockey, Stablehand, and Track Rider are some of the popular job roles in the racing department. These roles require a deep understanding of horse racing and a passion for the sport.

Operations

Operations department is responsible for the smooth functioning of the club. Job roles in this department include Facility Manager, Security Officer, and Maintenance Technician.

Finance

The finance department is responsible for managing the financial operations of the club. Job roles in this department include Accountant and Financial Analyst.

Marketing

Marketing department is responsible for promoting the club and its events. Job roles in this department include Sales Representative, Event Coordinator, and Marketing Manager.

Customer Service

Customer Service department is responsible for providing excellent service to the club's customers. Job roles in this department include Front Desk Receptionist, Ticketing Officer, and Call Center Representative.

Benefits

Singapore Turf Club offers a range of benefits to its employees, including:

Competitive Salary

The company provides a competitive salary package that is commensurate with the employee's experience and skill level.

Medical Benefits

The company provides medical benefits to its employees, including health insurance and dental care.

Training and Development

The company provides training and development opportunities to its employees to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Work-Life Balance

The company promotes work-life balance and encourages its employees to maintain a healthy balance between work and personal life.

Conclusion

Working at Singapore Turf Club is an exciting and rewarding experience. The company provides its employees with a dynamic and challenging work environment, competitive salary, and a range of benefits. If you are passionate about the racing industry and looking for an opportunity to grow your career, Singapore Turf Club could be the perfect place for you.
