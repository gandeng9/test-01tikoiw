Introduction

Tulsa Community College is a public community college in Tulsa, Oklahoma. The college has been providing quality education to students for over 50 years. TCC is committed to providing access to higher education and workforce development opportunities for all students. If you are looking for job openings in Tulsa, then TCC might be the perfect place for you.

Why work at Tulsa Community College?

TCC offers a supportive and inclusive work environment. The college is committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion. TCC provides its employees with excellent benefits, including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. The college also offers professional development opportunities to its employees to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Job Opportunities

TCC is currently hiring for various positions, including faculty, staff, and administrative roles. The college is looking for individuals who are passionate about education and can contribute to its mission. The job openings at TCC range from teaching positions in various subjects to administrative roles in finance, IT, and marketing.

Faculty Positions

TCC is hiring faculty members in various subjects, including English, mathematics, science, and social sciences. The college is looking for individuals who have a passion for teaching and can inspire students to learn. The faculty members at TCC are responsible for creating and delivering quality education to students.

Staff Positions

TCC is also hiring staff members in various departments, including admissions, financial aid, and student services. The college is looking for individuals who can provide excellent customer service to students and can work collaboratively with other staff members. The staff members at TCC play a crucial role in providing support to students and ensuring their success.

Administrative Positions

TCC is hiring for administrative positions in various departments, including finance, IT, and marketing. The college is looking for individuals who can provide leadership and support to the college's operations. The administrative positions at TCC are responsible for managing the college's resources and ensuring its success.

Application Process

If you are interested in any of the job openings at TCC, you can apply online through the college's website. The application process includes submitting a resume and cover letter. The college will review your application and contact you if you are selected for an interview.

Conclusion

Tulsa Community College is a great place to work if you are passionate about education and want to make a difference in students' lives. The college provides excellent benefits, professional development opportunities, and a supportive work environment. If you are looking for job openings in Tulsa, then TCC might be the perfect place for you. Apply now and join the TCC family.
