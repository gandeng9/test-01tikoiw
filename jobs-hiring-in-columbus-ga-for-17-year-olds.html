Introduction

Are you a 17-year-old looking for a job in Columbus, GA? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will provide you with a list of companies that are hiring 17-year-olds in Columbus, GA. We have also included some tips that can help you land a job.

Why Should 17 Year Olds Work?

Working at a young age has several benefits. It can help you develop new skills, gain valuable work experience, and earn some extra cash. Moreover, it can also boost your confidence and help you become more independent.

Companies Hiring 17 Year Olds in Columbus GA

Here are some companies that are hiring 17-year-olds in Columbus, GA:

1. Chick-fil-A

Chick-fil-A is a fast-food chain that is known for its delicious chicken sandwiches. They offer part-time jobs for 17-year-olds, and the pay is competitive.

2. Target

Target is a popular retail store that hires 17-year-olds for various positions, such as cashier, sales associate, and stocker. They offer flexible schedules and competitive pay.

3. McDonald's

McDonald's is another fast-food chain that hires 17-year-olds. They offer flexible schedules, on-the-job training, and opportunities for advancement.

4. Publix

Publix is a supermarket chain that hires 17-year-olds for various positions, such as bagger, cashier, and stocker. They offer competitive pay, flexible schedules, and opportunities for advancement.

Tips for Landing a Job

Here are some tips that can help you land a job:

1. Create a Resume

Creating a resume can help you stand out from other applicants. Include your education, work experience, and any relevant skills.

2. Dress Professionally

Dressing professionally can make a good impression on the employer. Wear a clean and ironed outfit that is appropriate for the job.

3. Be Prepared for the Interview

Research the company and be prepared to answer questions about the job. Also, bring a copy of your resume and a pen.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are several companies in Columbus, GA that are hiring 17-year-olds. Use the tips mentioned in this article to land a job and gain valuable work experience. Good luck!
