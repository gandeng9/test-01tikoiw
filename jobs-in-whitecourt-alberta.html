The Overview of Whitecourt, Alberta

Whitecourt is a town in the central part of Alberta, Canada. It is located approximately 177 km northwest of Edmonton, and it is home to approximately 10,000 residents. The town is situated in the heart of the forestry industry, and it is a hub for the oil and gas industry.

The Economy of Whitecourt

Whitecourt’s economy is largely driven by the forestry, oil, and gas industries. The town is home to a number of sawmills, and it is the location of one of the largest pulp and paper mills in North America. In addition, there are several oil and gas companies that operate in the area.

The Job Market in Whitecourt

The job market in Whitecourt is diverse, with opportunities in a variety of industries. The forestry, oil, and gas industries are major employers in the town, but there are also opportunities in healthcare, education, retail, and hospitality.
