Introduction

Looking for a job in the healthcare industry? Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich might be the perfect place for you! History of Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich

Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich has a rich history dating back to 1901. Originally known as the Woolwich Infirmary, the hospital was later renamed Queen Elizabeth Hospital in honor of the Queen Mother. Today, the hospital is a part of the Lewisham and Greenwich NHS Trust and serves the communities of Greenwich and Bexley.

Types of Jobs Available

Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich offers a wide range of employment opportunities for healthcare professionals. Some of the most common job titles at the hospital include:
  • Nurses
  • Doctors
  • Healthcare Assistants
  • Administrative Staff
  • Pharmacists

Nurses

Nurses are an essential part of the healthcare team at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich. They provide care for patients in a variety of settings, including the emergency department, intensive care unit, and general wards.

Doctors

Doctors at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich are responsible for diagnosing and treating patients. They work in a variety of specialties, including cardiology, gastroenterology, and neurology.

Healthcare Assistants

Healthcare assistants provide support to nurses and doctors by assisting with patient care. Their duties may include taking vital signs, assisting with personal hygiene, and helping patients with mobility.

Administrative Staff

Administrative staff at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich play a vital role in keeping the hospital running smoothly. They handle tasks such as scheduling appointments, managing patient records, and billing insurance companies.

Pharmacists

Pharmacists at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich are responsible for dispensing medication and ensuring that patients receive the correct dosage. They also provide advice on the safe use of medication and potential side effects.

Requirements for Jobs at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich

The requirements for jobs at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Woolwich vary depending on the position.
