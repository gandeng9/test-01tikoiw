Airport Jobs In Guwahati For 12Th Pass Candidates

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Guwahati, the gateway to the Northeast, is the largest city in Assam and is home to the Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport. With the increase in air travel, there has been a surge in demand for airport jobs in Guwahati. Many 12th pass candidates are seeking employment opportunities in this sector, and this article aims to provide information about the available job roles and requirements.

Job Roles

There are various job roles available for 12th pass candidates in the airport sector. Some of the most common roles include ground staff, security personnel, customer service executives, and baggage handlers. The ground staff is responsible for ensuring the smooth functioning of airport operations, including check-in, boarding, and luggage handling. The security personnel ensures the safety and security of passengers and airport property. Customer service executives assist passengers with their queries and provide them with relevant information.

Requirements

To apply for airport jobs in Guwahati, candidates must fulfill certain requirements. For most roles, candidates must have completed their 12th standard education. They must also possess good communication skills, be physically fit, and have a pleasing personality. Language proficiency in English and Hindi is an added advantage. Additionally, candidates must be willing to work in shifts, including night shifts.

Training

Most airport jobs in Guwahati require candidates to undergo training before they can start working. The training program covers various aspects such as airport operations, safety and security, customer service, and baggage handling. The duration of the training program varies depending on the job role and can range from a few weeks to a few months.

Salary and Benefits

The salary and benefits offered to airport employees in Guwahati vary depending on the job role and experience. On average, a ground staff employee can earn between INR 15,000 to INR 25,000 per month. Security personnel can earn between INR 12,000 to INR 20,000 per month. Customer service executives can earn between INR 18,000 to INR 30,000 per month. In addition to the salary, employees are entitled to benefits such as medical insurance, provident fund, and paid leave.

How to Apply

To apply for airport jobs in Guwahati, candidates can visit the airport's official website or job portals such as Naukri.com and Indeed.com. Candidates can search for job openings based on their preferred job role and apply online by submitting their resumes and other necessary documents. Candidates can also visit the airport's HR department to inquire about job openings and submit their resumes in person.

Conclusion

The airport sector in Guwahati is growing rapidly, and there are ample job opportunities for 12th pass candidates. With the right qualifications and skills, candidates can secure a well-paying job with various benefits. The training provided by the airport authorities can also help candidates enhance their skills and knowledge in this sector. So, if you are a 12th pass candidate looking for a job, consider exploring the airport sector in Guwahati.
