Jobs In Jaipur Airport For 12Th Pass: Opportunities And Requirements

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

If you are a 12th pass job seeker from Jaipur and looking for a promising career in the aviation industry, then Jaipur Airport could be your destination. The airport, which is also known as the Sanganer Airport, offers various job opportunities for 12th pass candidates in different departments. In this article, we will discuss the available jobs, requirements, and growth opportunities in detail.

Available Jobs

Jaipur Airport offers jobs in various departments such as ground staff, security, cargo, and customer service. The ground staff department includes jobs like baggage handling, ticketing, and check-in. The security department offers jobs like security personnel, screening officers, and security supervisors. The cargo department includes jobs like cargo handlers and supervisors. The customer service department offers jobs like customer service executives, information desk executives, and hospitality staff.

Requirements

To apply for the jobs mentioned above, the candidate must fulfill certain criteria. The candidate must be a 12th pass from a recognized board or university. The candidate must be fluent in English and Hindi languages. The candidate must have good communication skills, both verbal and written. The candidate must have basic computer knowledge. The candidate must be physically fit and have a presentable personality.

Selection Process

The selection process for the jobs in Jaipur airport includes a written test, physical test, and interview. The written test consists of questions related to general knowledge, English language, and basic mathematics. The physical test includes tasks like baggage lifting, running, and standing for long hours. The interview is conducted to assess the candidate's communication skills, personality, and confidence.

Salary and Benefits

The salary for the jobs in Jaipur airport varies depending on the department and job profile. The starting salary for ground staff and customer service executives is around Rs. 15,000 per month. The starting salary for security personnel and cargo handlers is around Rs. 18,000 per month. The benefits include health insurance, provident fund, and travel allowances. The employees also get opportunities for career growth and training programs.

Conclusion

In conclusion, jobs in Jaipur airport for 12th pass candidates provide a promising career opportunity in the aviation industry. The airport offers jobs in different departments like ground staff, security, cargo, and customer service. The selection process includes a written test, physical test, and interview. The salary and benefits are attractive, and the employees also get opportunities for career growth. So, if you are a 12th pass job seeker from Jaipur, grab this opportunity and start your journey towards a successful career.
