Introduction

Are you a 12th pass student looking for part-time jobs in Zirakpur? Whether you are a student or a job seeker, part-time jobs can be an excellent way to earn some extra income. In this article, we will discuss the top part-time jobs in Zirakpur that are suitable for 12th pass students.

Retail Jobs

Retail jobs are one of the most popular part-time jobs in Zirakpur, especially for those who are 12th pass. These jobs are suitable for students who are looking for part-time work to earn some extra income. Retail jobs in Zirakpur include salesperson, cashier, and customer service representative.

Delivery jobs are another popular part-time job in Zirakpur. These jobs are suitable for students who have a two-wheeler and are looking for flexible work hours. Delivery jobs in Zirakpur include food delivery, courier delivery, and e-commerce delivery.

If you are good at a subject and have a passion for teaching, tutoring jobs can be an excellent option for you. These jobs are suitable for 12th pass students who are good at one or more subjects. Tutoring jobs in Zirakpur include home tuition, online tuition, and coaching center jobs.

Event Management Jobs

Event management jobs are suitable for students who are good at organizing events and have good communication skills. Event management jobs in Zirakpur include event coordinator, event planner, and event assistant.

Freelancing Jobs

Freelancing jobs are suitable for students who have a specific skill set and are looking for part-time work. Part-time jobs in Zirakpur are an excellent way for 12th pass students to earn some extra income.
