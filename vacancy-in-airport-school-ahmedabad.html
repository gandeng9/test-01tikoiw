Vacancy In Airport School Ahmedabad

Best jobs Tips and References website . Search anything about jobs Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Airport School Ahmedabad has recently announced vacancies for various positions in their institution. This news has come as a great opportunity for those who are looking for a job in the education sector. The school is well known for providing quality education to its students and has been consistently ranked among the top schools in Ahmedabad.

Positions Available

The school is looking to fill positions for teachers, administrative staff, and support staff. For teachers, the vacancies are available for various subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Hindi. The school is also looking for administrative staff to handle various tasks such as admissions, accounts, and HR. Support staff positions include housekeeping, security, and maintenance.

Qualifications Required

For the teaching positions, the applicants must have a Bachelor's or Master's degree in the relevant subject along with a B.Ed. degree. They should also have at least 2-3 years of teaching experience in a reputed school. Administrative staff should have a graduation degree and relevant work experience. Support staff should have passed at least 10th standard.

Application Procedure

Interested candidates can apply for the vacancies by sending their resumes to the school's email address. The school will shortlist the candidates and call them for an interview. The final selection will be based on the candidate's performance in the interview.

Salary and Benefits

The school offers a competitive salary package along with various benefits such as health insurance, provident fund, and paid leaves. Teachers also get opportunities for professional development through workshops and seminars.

Tips for Applying

1. Research the School

Before applying for the vacancies, it is important to research the school and its culture. This will give you an idea of what the school is looking for in its employees and whether you will be a good fit for the institution.

2. Customize Your Resume

Tailor your resume to the job description and highlight your relevant skills and experiences. This will increase your chances of getting shortlisted for an interview.

3. Prepare for the Interview

Prepare for the interview by researching common interview questions and practicing your responses. Dress professionally and arrive on time for the interview.

Conclusion

Airport School Ahmedabad is a reputed institution that provides quality education to its students. The vacancies announced by the school provide a great opportunity for those who are looking for a job in the education sector. Interested candidates should apply for the vacancies and follow the tips mentioned above to increase their chances of getting selected.
