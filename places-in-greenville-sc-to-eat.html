Introduction

Are you planning a visit to Greenville, SC and looking for some great places to eat? Look no further! This charming southern city is known for its vibrant food scene, where you can find everything from traditional southern cuisine to international dishes. In this blog post, we will explore 10 of the best places to eat in Greenville, SC.

The Anchorage

The Anchorage is a popular restaurant located in the West End of downtown Greenville. The menu is inspired by seasonal ingredients and features a variety of dishes from small plates to entrees. The restaurant also offers a great selection of cocktails and wine.

The Lazy Goat

The Lazy Goat is a Mediterranean-inspired restaurant located on the Reedy River in downtown Greenville. The menu features a variety of tapas-style dishes, as well as larger entrees. The restaurant also has a great outdoor seating area with beautiful views of the river.

Husk Greenville

Husk Greenville is a modern southern restaurant located in a historic building in downtown Greenville. The menu features traditional southern dishes with a modern twist, using locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant also has a great selection of craft cocktails.

Passerelle Bistro

Passerelle Bistro is a French-inspired restaurant located on the Reedy River in downtown Greenville. The menu features classic French dishes, as well as some southern-inspired options. The restaurant also has a great outdoor seating area with beautiful views of the river.

Pomegranate on Main

Pomegranate on Main is a Mediterranean-inspired restaurant located in downtown Greenville. The menu features a variety of dishes from small plates to entrees, as well as a great selection of wine. The restaurant also has a beautiful outdoor seating area.

Up on the Roof

Up on the Roof is a rooftop bar and restaurant located in the heart of downtown Greenville. The menu features a variety of small plates and entrees, as well as a great selection of craft cocktails and beer. The restaurant also offers stunning views of the city.

OJ’s Diner

OJ’s Diner is a classic southern diner located in Greenville. The menu features traditional southern dishes such as biscuits and gravy, fried chicken, and collard greens. The restaurant also offers a great selection of breakfast items.

The Chocolate Moose Bakery

The Chocolate Moose Bakery is a popular bakery located in the heart of downtown Greenville. The bakery offers a variety of desserts and pastries, as well as sandwiches and coffee. The bakery is also known for its custom cakes.

Smoke on the Water

Smoke on the Water is a southern barbecue restaurant located in downtown Greenville. The menu features a variety of smoked meats, as well as southern sides such as mac and cheese and collard greens. The restaurant also has a great selection of craft beer.

The Trappe Door

The Trappe Door is a Belgian-inspired restaurant located in downtown Greenville. The menu features classic Belgian dishes, as well as a great selection of Belgian beer. The restaurant also has a cozy atmosphere with a fireplace and outdoor seating area.

Conclusion

Greenville, SC is a foodie’s paradise with a variety of great restaurants to choose from. Whether you’re in the mood for traditional southern cuisine or international dishes, there’s something for everyone. We hope this list of 10 of the best places to eat in Greenville, SC has inspired you to try some new restaurants during your next visit.
