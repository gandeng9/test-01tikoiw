Introduction to PAF

Pakistan Air Force (PAF) is the aerial warfare branch of Pakistan Armed Forces which was established in 1947 after the Partition of India. PAF is one of the well-organized and equipped air forces in the world. It is also known as the guardians of the skies.

What are Airmen Jobs?

Airmen jobs refer to the technical and non-technical jobs in PAF. These jobs are offered to young and energetic individuals who want to serve their country and join PAF as a career.

Latest Airmen Jobs in PAF

PAF offers a wide range of airmen jobs every year. In 2023, PAF is offering following airmen jobs:

Aircraft Maintenance Jobs

Aircraft maintenance jobs are technical jobs offered by PAF. These jobs require an individual to maintain the aircrafts and ensure that they are in perfect condition for flying. The job requires technical skills and knowledge of aircraft maintenance.

Ground Support Jobs

Ground support jobs are non-technical jobs offered by PAF. These jobs require an individual to provide support on the ground to the aircrafts and their crew. The job requires physical fitness and the ability to work in tough conditions.

Air Traffic Control Jobs

Air Traffic Control (ATC) jobs are technical jobs offered by PAF. These jobs require an individual to manage the air traffic and ensure the safety of the aircrafts in the air. The job requires technical skills and knowledge of ATC procedures.

Logistics Jobs

Logistics jobs are non-technical jobs offered by PAF. These jobs require an individual to manage the logistics and supplies of PAF. The job requires organizational skills and the ability to work in a team.

Administration Jobs

Administration jobs are non-technical jobs offered by PAF. These jobs require an individual to manage the administrative tasks of PAF. The job requires communication skills and the ability to work in a team.

Eligibility Criteria for Airmen Jobs in PAF

To apply for airmen jobs in PAF, an individual must meet the following eligibility criteria:

Age Limit

The age limit for airmen jobs in PAF is 16 to 22 years.

Education

The education requirement for airmen jobs in PAF varies from job to job. However, the minimum education requirement is matriculation.

Physical Fitness

Physical fitness is a must for airmen jobs in PAF. An individual must meet the required physical standards to be eligible for these jobs.

How to Apply for Airmen Jobs in PAF?

To apply for airmen jobs in PAF, an individual can visit the PAF website or the nearest PAF recruitment center. The application process includes written tests, interviews, and medical tests.
