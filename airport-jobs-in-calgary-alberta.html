A Guide To Airport Jobs In Calgary, Alberta In 2023

Introduction

Are you looking for a rewarding career in the aviation industry in Calgary, Alberta? Working at the airport can provide you with a variety of exciting job opportunities. Calgary International Airport (YYC) is one of Canada's busiest airports and is constantly expanding, which means there are plenty of job openings available. In this article, we'll explore the different types of airport jobs in Calgary and what it takes to land one.

Types of Airport Jobs

There are a wide range of airport jobs available, from entry-level positions to highly specialized roles. Some of the most common airport jobs in Calgary include:

1. Customer Service Representative

As a customer service representative, you'll be responsible for assisting passengers with their travel needs, including check-in, baggage handling, and boarding. You'll need strong communication and problem-solving skills, as well as the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

2. Security Officer

Security officers are responsible for ensuring the safety and security of passengers, employees, and airport property. You'll need to be able to work well under pressure and have excellent attention to detail.

3. Air Traffic Controller

Air traffic controllers are responsible for directing the movement of aircraft in and around the airport. This is a highly specialized role that requires extensive training and certification.

4. Maintenance Technician

Maintenance technicians are responsible for keeping the airport's facilities and equipment in good working order. This includes everything from repairing escalators and elevators to maintaining the runways and taxiways.

Qualifications and Requirements

The qualifications and requirements for airport jobs in Calgary vary depending on the position. However, some general requirements include: - Minimum age of 18 years old - High school diploma or equivalent - Ability to pass a security clearance - Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings, weekends, and holidays

How to Apply

If you're interested in applying for an airport job in Calgary, the first step is to visit the airport's website and search for job openings. You can also check with individual airlines and companies that operate at the airport. Be sure to tailor your resume and cover letter to the specific job you're applying for and highlight any relevant experience or skills.

Benefits of Working at the Airport

Working at the airport can provide a number of benefits, including: - Competitive salaries and benefits packages - Opportunities for career advancement - Exposure to a diverse range of people and cultures - Access to travel benefits and discounts

Conclusion

Whether you're just starting your career or looking for a change, working at the airport in Calgary can provide you with a rewarding and exciting job. With a wide range of job opportunities available and constant expansion, there's never been a better time to explore airport jobs in Calgary.
