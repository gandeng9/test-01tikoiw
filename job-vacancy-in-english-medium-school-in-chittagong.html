Job Vacancy In English Medium School In Chittagong

Introduction

Are you looking for a job in an English medium school in Chittagong? Well, you are in luck! There are several job vacancies available in some of the most reputable schools in the city. In this article, we will discuss the job opportunities, requirements, and benefits of working in an English medium school in Chittagong.

What is an English Medium School?

An English medium school is a school where the primary language of instruction is English. These schools follow the curriculum of international education systems, such as Cambridge International Examinations, International Baccalaureate, or Edexcel. The goal of English medium schools is to provide students with a high-quality education in English, preparing them for higher education and future careers.

Job Opportunities

There are several job opportunities available in English medium schools in Chittagong. These include teaching positions, administrative positions, and support staff positions. Teaching positions are available for subjects such as English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Foreign Languages. Administrative positions include positions such as Principals, Vice-Principals, and Department Heads. Support staff positions include positions such as Librarians, Nurses, and IT Technicians.

Requirements

To work in an English medium school in Chittagong, you must have a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Education or a related field. You must also have a teaching certification, such as a Teaching Certificate or a Teaching License. Additionally, you must have excellent English language skills, both written and verbal.

Benefits

Working in an English medium school in Chittagong comes with several benefits. These include competitive salaries, health insurance, retirement plans, and professional development opportunities. English medium schools also provide a multicultural environment, allowing you to work with students and colleagues from different cultures and backgrounds.

How to Apply?

To apply for a job in an English medium school in Chittagong, you must first check the school's website or contact the school's Human Resources Department. You will be required to submit your resume, cover letter, and teaching certification. If you meet the requirements, you will be invited for an interview, which may include a teaching demonstration or a written test.

Conclusion

Working in an English medium school in Chittagong can be a fulfilling and rewarding experience. With several job opportunities available, competitive salaries, and a multicultural environment, it is an excellent place to start or continue your teaching career. If you meet the requirements, don't hesitate to apply for a job today!
