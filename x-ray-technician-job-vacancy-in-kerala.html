X Ray Technician Job Vacancy In Kerala

Introduction

Kerala, a state in southern India, is known for its beautiful beaches, serene backwaters, and lush greenery. It is also home to several top-notch hospitals and medical centers. With the increasing demand for healthcare services, there is a growing need for qualified healthcare professionals, including X-ray technicians. In this article, we will discuss the job vacancy for X-ray technicians in Kerala.

What is an X Ray Technician?

An X-ray technician is a healthcare professional who specializes in taking X-ray images of patients. They operate X-ray machines, position patients for imaging procedures, and ensure that the images produced are of high quality. X-ray technicians work in hospitals, clinics, and imaging centers.

Job Vacancy for X Ray Technicians in Kerala

There is currently a high demand for X-ray technicians in Kerala. Many hospitals and clinics are looking for qualified individuals to fill their job vacancies. The job market for X-ray technicians in Kerala is expected to grow in the coming years due to the increasing demand for healthcare services.

Qualifications Required

To become an X-ray technician in Kerala, you need to have a diploma or degree in radiography or medical imaging technology from a recognized institution. You also need to be registered with the Kerala Paramedical Council. Some hospitals and clinics may require additional certifications or training.

Job Responsibilities

The job responsibilities of an X-ray technician in Kerala include operating X-ray machines, positioning patients for imaging procedures, maintaining patient records, ensuring the safety of patients and staff, and maintaining equipment and supplies.

Salary and Benefits

The salary of an X-ray technician in Kerala varies depending on the employer, experience, and qualifications. On average, X-ray technicians in Kerala earn between INR 15,000 to INR 30,000 per month. Some hospitals and clinics may offer additional benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

Advancement Opportunities

X-ray technicians in Kerala have several opportunities for career advancement. With additional training and certifications, they can become specialized in areas such as MRI, CT scans, and mammography. They can also move into supervisory or managerial roles.

Conclusion

The job vacancy for X-ray technicians in Kerala is growing due to the increasing demand for healthcare services. To become an X-ray technician in Kerala, you need to have the required qualifications and be registered with the Kerala Paramedical Council. X-ray technicians in Kerala have several opportunities for career advancement and can earn a competitive salary with additional benefits.
