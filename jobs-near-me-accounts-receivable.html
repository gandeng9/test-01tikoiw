What is Accounts Receivable?

Accounts Receivable is a term used in accounting to refer to money that is owed to a company by its customers for goods or services that have been delivered but not yet paid for. It is an important aspect of any business as it represents the money that the company expects to receive in the near future.

Accounts Receivable Jobs Near Me

If you are interested in pursuing a career in the field of Accounts Receivable, there are a number of jobs available in your local area. These jobs include positions such as Accounts Receivable Clerk, Accounts Receivable Specialist, and Accounts Receivable Manager.

Accounts Receivable Clerk

An Accounts Receivable Clerk is responsible for maintaining and updating the company's Accounts Receivable records. This includes tracking customer payments, sending out invoices, and following up on any outstanding payments.

Accounts Receivable Specialist

An Accounts Receivable Specialist is responsible for managing the company's Accounts Receivable department. This includes overseeing the Accounts Receivable Clerk, ensuring that all customer payments are processed in a timely manner, and communicating with customers regarding their outstanding balances.

Accounts Receivable Manager

An Accounts Receivable Manager is responsible for overseeing the entire Accounts Receivable department. This includes managing the Accounts Receivable Specialist, ensuring that all customer payments are processed accurately and on time, and developing strategies to improve the company's cash flow.
