2. Department Manager

As a department manager, you'll be responsible for overseeing a specific department within the store. You'll be responsible for managing employees, ordering inventory, and ensuring the department meets its sales goals.

As an assistant store manager, you'll be responsible for assisting the store manager in day-to-day operations. You'll be responsible for managing employees, ensuring the store meets its sales goals, and maintaining customer satisfaction.
