Introduction

If you're considering becoming a nurse practitioner (NP), you're probably wondering which programs are the best fit for you. 1. Duke University

Duke University offers a highly regarded part-time NP program that can be completed in two years. This program is designed for working professionals who want to earn their degree while maintaining their current job. The curriculum includes courses in advanced practice nursing, patient care management, and health policy. Students can choose to specialize in adult-gerontology, family, or pediatric primary care.

Why Choose Duke?

Duke University is known for its excellent nursing programs and highly trained faculty. The university is committed to providing students with a rigorous curriculum that prepares them for a successful career in nursing. In addition, Duke offers flexible scheduling options that allow students to balance their studies with their work and personal life.
